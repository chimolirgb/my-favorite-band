Favorite band

Sauti-sol is a Kenyan Afro-Pop band formed in 2005 by Bien Baraza, Austin Chimano Savara Mugidi and Polycarp Otieno. It is one of the best bands ever in Africa with amazing music hits. Here is a short Biography about sauti-sol band members. Bien Baraza:He is a vocalist,songwriter and guitarist whose style of singing is soulful and his songwriting skills are celebrated by many. Austin Chimano:He is a vocalist keystarand saxophone player whose deep voice and flamboyant style makes him stand out in the group. Savara Mudigi: He is a vocalist, percussionist, drummer and producer whose vasality in the band has played an instrumental role during tour and establishment of labels. Polycarp Otieno: He is a guitarist and producer whose signature style guitar riffs blends harmonies and drum rhythm
